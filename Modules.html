Build your best team to beat a raid boss!

Enter the raid boss information on the main simulator page first, then hit the "Find Counters" button in this tool.

Your best Pokemon against the raid boss will be shown on the left! A sample battle party is given in the Party Optimizer.

Drag the Pokemon up/down to adjust the order and see how the battle party's performance changes. Hit "Permutate Party" to find the best performing one.

You can also modify the members of the battle party by dragging the Pokemon from Pokemon Pool to Party Optimizer or vice versa.
